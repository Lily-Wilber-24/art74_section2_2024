In order to make sure the painting was accurate I used something called gridding for my sketch. Gridding is when you draw a grid on an image and then recreate that grid on a larger scale. You then transfer the contents of each individual box from the smaller image to the larger recreation. I added some fungi and plants to contribute to the forest spirit look I was going for.
